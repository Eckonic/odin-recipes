Lasagne

Home

Description:

Lasagne is both a type of pasta and an Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with a savory filling, typically a meat or vegetable ragù, a béchamel (white sauce), and cheese, which is then baked in the oven.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Pasta plates

Steps:

  1. Fry ground beef
  2. Dice the onion
  3. Add Tomato paste
  4. Add Pasta plates
